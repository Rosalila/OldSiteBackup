Surprise! It's Halloween at Yamaku School! 

You are invited to Katawa Shoujo costume party at Yamaku school, do you want to attend? "Dive Kick! Katawa Shoujo: Halloween Surprise Edition 2012" from the creators of Katawa Dive Kick! is a surprise for all the Katawa Shoujo Fans. We want to thank all the contributors, testers that send us feedback (via issue report or comments) and all the players.

Characters

This time each girl has different skills and costumes.

  • Shizune as a SM Queen - Punish with her whip.
  • Hanako as a Witch - Throws 3 magical fireballs.
  • Lilly as a Maid - Invokes a big cup of tea.
  • Emi as a Bunny - Invokes spinning carrots all over the screen.
  • Rin as Question - Throws her fast and powerful question insignia.

Contributors

Rosalila Studio is working with the KSFG developers in different areas such as graphics arts, music, coding, character mechanics.
