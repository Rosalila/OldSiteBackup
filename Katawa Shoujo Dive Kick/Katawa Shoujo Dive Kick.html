What is Katawa Dive Kick?

Katawa Dive Kick is a game inspired by Katawa Shoujo (by 4Leaf Studios) and Dive Kick (by One True Game Studios). This game is a demo of Katawa Shoujo Fighing Game (KSFG) who has the approval of 4Leaf Studios. 

  

Video Sample

 

Characters

The current playable characters are the five main girls in the game each one with different  abilities.

  • Shizune - Balanced
  • Hanako - Special shy jump after the dive kick
  • Lilly - Slow, big range
  • Emi - Speed, low range
  • Rin - Cycles 3 diferent dive kicks

Contributors

KSFG developers contributed with the graphic arts and Rosalila Studio with the music, engine code and the characters mechanics.

Licences

As the original Katawa Shoujo art is under Creative Commons and the engine (Ren'py) is free software, we also work with similar licences.

History

Katawa Dive Kick idea was born in the KSFG developer team in september 2012, a month later a first release was made as a demo with the goal of finding more contributors for KSFG.
